Overview

As and when a sale happens; Skyfinch's Point of Sale (PoS) solution, tracks, records, accounts, and updates all essential business workflow components involved with the product sold. Item Master, supporting multiple 'Unit of Measurements' (UoM), helps the listing of products under fitting headers; and the Item Groups provide further clarity by grouping the listed products by kind.... The PoS system's integration with the inventory and accounting modules automatically updates inventory status and performs all basic accounting for the sale to keep track of business cash flow. Role-specific user access to the system ensures data security. The module comes with the facility to performer ustomer-specific calculation of Loyalty Bonus to appropriate or deduct loyalty points. Themodule supports businesses to use add-on devices like barcode scanners, barcode printers, and receipt/invoice printers (all types of printers) at the billing stations. The following are the features that constitute the Skyfinch PoS software system to enable business stakeholders to make best decisions for the sales/purchase of their business related products:

Inventory Management

  • The tenacity of the module to accommodate more than 100,000 Stock Keeping Units (SKUs), enables the effortless and efficient management of large inventories with diverse product categories.

  • The system is customizable to design the categories and sub-categories of the Inventory to have a better understanding and visibility through personalized screens.

  • Any change in the category level of products can be easily incorporated into the solution.
