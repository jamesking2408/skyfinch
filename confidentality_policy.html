Non-personal data
  • This website, like most websites, stores information in small data files called cookies on your computer. These cookies are used by this website in several ways including enabling certain features, remembering choices that you make, improving the efficiency of the Site, and to provide to us information about how people use the Site so that we can improve it.
  • This website’s cookies will either be deleted or not deleted automatically when you close your browser depending upon the need to retain the information in the cookie between your visits to this website. If you do not allow this website to use cookies, then certain features or sections of the Site may not be available to you or may not function as expected.Cookies can also be removed or blocked by following the help directions in your browser. Rane Websites use these temporary cookies which will expire and get erased when you close your browser session and persistent cookies which stay in your browser until they expire, or until you delete them. We use the following types of cookies:
  • 3.1 Essential cookies: enable services and applications you have specifically asked for. These types of cookies enable you to log into secure areas of our Rane Websites to provide the necessary security and to ensure quick loading of the content of the webpages. The use of these cookies implicates processing the anonymous browser session ID, permission groups and User ID, if any.
  • 3.2 Performance/Analytics cookies: collects aggregated and pseudonymous information about the web pages you visit and don’t collect information that identifies you as an individual. These cookies collect information about how visitors use our Rane Websites, for example, which pages our visitors refer most often, and whether they get error messages on those pages. We do not associate these data with personal data such as your name or email address, which you may have provided.
  • 3.3 Third party cookies: collect information for third parties. For the purpose of embedding video clips into our Rane Websites, we use services provided by YouTube [www.youtube.com]. By default, the YouTube video player does not use any cookies. The cookies, if any, used by third party plugins are controlled by the third parties. Please check with respective third party websites for a description of the cookies used in their plugins.
  • As far as this data must be considered personal data, the processing is based on applicable statutory provisions which justify this on the ground that the processing is necessary to provide the requested services from Rane Websites, or the data is used in pseudonymous form for the purpose of marketing and optimization and the User has not objected to this use.
