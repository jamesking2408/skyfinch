About Us

We Help Our Clients To Grow Their Business

The promoters of Skyfinch are an ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 27001:2013 certified company, headed by business professionals having more than 20 years of experience in various business domains. Since its establishment in the year 2004 at Puducherry, India.

     Powered by a team of technically certified and highly skilled IT professionals Skyfinch provides customized, best-fit, latest technology solutions. Our digital solutions, aptly supported by our customized mobile application services, address the unique challenges of the business requirements of each of our clients. We have successfully accomplished over 200 implementations across a plethora of business types through our time tested implementation methodology.

     Our vision is to give innovative, configurable, flexible, cost-effective digital solutions to all types of industries to enable businesses to save time, increase productivity and maximize return-on-investment.
     We distinguish ourselves from our competitors through quality products; dedicated service; quick response to the clients' needs; domain proficiency; operating methodology; affordability; and flexibility in working with our clients.

     Our mission is to provide durable and easy to use solutions to our customers; and be able to surpass our customers' expectations through integration and innovation.
     As experts in offering software solutions, we adopt proven practices and methodologies right from requirement definition to final handover; and strive to increase the productivity of our customers' businesses through process integration, transformation, cost reduction, and process re-engineering.
