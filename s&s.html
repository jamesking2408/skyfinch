Overview

     The Skyfinch Sales & Service module gives total visibility into the entire business process and maximizes the performance efficiency. The tracking and monitoring facility of the module accounts for each and every spare purchased, replaced, and sold. The software is the best-fit solution for computer showrooms, automobile showrooms, CCTV shops, electronics repair shops, water purifier shops, two wheeler showrooms, car service showrooms, and more.

Modules

Service Management

Skyfinch Service-CRM's single interface facilitates the creation of a systematic pricing quotation immediately upon receipt of complaint. Once the customer approves the quote the task can be delegated to accomplish the service in a streamlined fashion. This enhances customer satisfaction by reducing turnaround times and by increasing the efficiency of service process.
The total integration of all the business processes involved in the service workflow gives business stakeholders to potentially:
Identify revenue generation;
Evaluate inventory consumption;
Evaluate manpower usage; and
Identify quality of the product

Purchase Management

POS enhances the agility of Purchase management by simplifying and rendering the Inward Entry procedure free of hassles
Accurate product costing is actuated by the modules capacity to build buying level product cost upon the Landing Price
The system is programmable to automatically project selling price updates according to any change attributed to marked price
Level of Stock is automatically updated as when Goods Inward are verified and accounted for in the system
The module's integration with inventory captures a clear vector of purchases and stock to accurately forecast the Profit/Gross margin
Provision to assign distributor-wise formula enables to arrive at efficient and reasonable distributor margins

Inventory Management

The tenacity of the module to accommodate more than 100,000 Stock Keeping Units (SKUs), enables the effortless and efficient management of large inventories with diverse product categories
The system is customizable to design the categories and sub-categories of the Inventory to have a better understanding and visibility through personalized screens
Any change in the category level of products can be easily incorporated into the solution
The module offers clear visibility of the rolled up totals for any category level of the Inventory
The Dynamic Drill Down view presents a vivid picture of the Inventory status
Actual Vs Estimated stock audits can be conducted at any time to easily and quickly find if there are discrepancies
Support of excel files while doing stock audits is a great advantage

Customer Management

Simple steps to create Customer Record enhances user-experience
Segmenting Customers based on various categories gives clarity in choosing the most effective area of focus for campaigns and reap maximum benefits
Tracking and maintaining customers' transaction level with the business empowers to assign customer-specific Credit Limits
The facility to track and monitor Receivables pending from customers presents customer-wise payment aging notifications, sends SMSs/emails regarding the dues to customers, and amicably enables the recovery of all pending payments.
The module comes with the faculty to handle time-bound package deals, Offers, Schemes, and customer-specific appropriation/deduction of Loyalty Bonus points

Supplier Management

Skyfinch offers customized solutions to suit the supplier relationship process requirements of any business. These solutions digitize and streamline the process of: Identifying, sourcing, contracting, and procuring the required goods/services from qualified suppliers Managing supplier performance Implementing technologies, processes, policies, and procedures to support the purchasing process Developing and maintaining relationship with suppliers towards healthy prospects Generating cost-effective forecasts/theories to meet any kind of supply and demand scenario

Reorder/Replenishment

Reorder/Replenishment notifications are raised to designated personnel based on:
Min/Max sales range for any particular date in the previous year(s).
Sales for a particular period (days, month, years) from the past.
Reorder Level specifications.
Lead times.
Fall in the prescribed safety-stock-level of any given product.

Barcode Stickers

Facilitates the designing and printing of barcode's indigenously.
Enables the generation of stickers using normal laser printer or ink jet printer with the liberty of choosing desired logo and design.
The system supports dedicated sticker printers, printing A4 size sticker sheets, and partial sheet printing.

Complaint Management

One of the key aspects in sales and service that maximizes customer satisfaction is Complaints Management. This plays a very important role in the retention and expansion of client-base. Skyfinch's Complaint Management module actuates the efficient and comprehensive tracking and monitoring of the service process and provides the scope to rectify discrepancies and constantly enhance efficiency of service by facilitating:
Generation of complaint ticket number;
Tracking of Activities;
Simple escalation procedure;
Analysis of the nature of discrepancies;
Accurate resolutions through time-tested reports; and
Initiating AMC procedures based on warranty status.

AMC Management

The solution provides a clear picture of the sales, warranty, and AMC scenario and raises notifications for Preventive Maintenance Schedule (PMS) visits
The module ensures the increase in service revenue, while also enhancing customer's confidence in the Product
The system generates Annual Maintenance Contract proposals by tracking the warranty and AMC expiry dates

Spare Part Consumption

The system helps to increase revenue from spare parts sales by accounting for each and every spare part purchased and sold
The facility to generate assign unique codes to spares gives clear visibility to track and monitor the consumption and warranty/AMC details of spare parts

Technician Allocation

Based on the nature of complaint and the equipment type the system prompts the allocation of the appropriate Technician for any given job
The Technician Allocation facility rules out logjams by seamlessly scheduling the job works and by sequentially delegating the work in a logical pattern
The solution prioritizes jobs based on customer-type and customer-request and ensures maximum customer satisfaction

SMS Support

The system's provision to facilitate the sending of invoices/receipts to clients in SMS mode renders the procedure paperless. Ability to support the same in any regional language is a great advantage.
Business stakeholders can receive periodical SMS reports on inventory status, daily sales, key accounting data, etc, and gain remote access to business data even through the use of a basic dongle.
Skyfinch integrates the SMS gateway provider with the any business using HTTP API.

Tax Management

Simple yet perfect tax definition empowers the accurate appropriation of GST components in a user-friendly fashion.
The flexibility of the system gives scope to enter and alter tax details for accuracy even during Invoice level.
Customized taxation module enables the assigning of taxes at multiple levels for the same product depending on the type of sale.
Comprehensive solution enables handling all attributes of taxation like, GST, SGST, CGST, IGST/UGST.
The scalability of the software is robust enough to accommodate all futuristic governmental shifts in taxation procedures.

Financial control with GST

All System required accounts are Auto Generated.
You can create any number of additional Financial Accounts.
Simplified import and export functionality eases the integration of all accounts and transactions in Tally with Skyfinch's accounting platform.
The flexibility of the solution provides the scope to view financial reports from multiple perspectives.
The solution capacitates the viewing of Composite Ledgers, and empowers business stakeholders to enjoy a robust Cash Register management system.

Analytical Reports

Skyfinch's business-specific customized solution, through multi-level data analytics empowers business owners and decision makers to have key business reports on:
The products that top the sales scenario.
Fast moving items.
Dump stock & dead stock.
Peak of business rush hour for effective and efficient staff management.
Daily sales statistics, sales returns, stock items and short stocks.

User control Management

Provision to assign computer system-specific grant/limit of access to business data restricts users from accessing data beyond the necessity of their professional scope.
Ability to create login IDs, user groups, functionality groups, and capture Action History help to manage access rights effectively.
The power to determine role-specific access to specific areas of the application and the tenacity to create additional passwords give total control over data security.
